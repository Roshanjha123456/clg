About us


WELCOME TO ADVANCED EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS and Management (AITM)

Advanced Institute of Technology and Management (AITM) was established in the year 2006 and is the first engineering college in Palwal District. It is a premier institute of northern India imparting quality education in various courses like B. Tech, M. Tech, MBA, BBA, MCA and BCA. AITM is approved by AICTE, MHRD (Govt. of India) and DTE (Haryana) and affiliated to J.C. Bose University of Science & Technology, YMCA, Faridabad. This institute is highly acclaimed in academic circles and by the students and the general public for its contribution to the society in producing top class professionals in the fields of engineering, technology and management. AITM has the most modern infrastructure, well-equipped laboratories having sophisticated instruments, air conditioned library, highly experienced and dedicated faculty members. AITM is proud to provide an atmosphere conducive to learning and is always bustling with academic and extra-curricular activities.
